Languages...?

I speak three languages fluently: 2 international and one local. On an intermediate level, I speak Korean. Although I studied French for 3 years in middle school, I only remember some basic words and songs we used to memorize. In highschool, I started developing a strange affection towards German language, so I taught myself that although my interest quickly diverted towards Spanish. Currently, I am studying Korean and Spanish in hopes of becoming a professional speaker of the languages.
