How I Started Writing On Quora

I have been a passive Quora user for quite a while, reading other peoples' theories and experiences. It was definitely a fun and rewarding experience without having to put myself on the spotlight. However, for a person who reads a lot, writing becomes an urging passion forcing its reader to share their view of the world. Hence, on the summer vacation of my freshman year of highschool, I wrote my first ever answer on Quora. The feedbacks, replies and compliments I got only pushed me to write further and on a consistent basis.

I document all my thoughts on my phone : Creepy?

In a world where nobody actually cares for other peoples' opinions especially that of younger ones, writing has been my safe resort. I write whatever thoughts that come to my mind that are worth writing. From the silliest 'the world is unfair' thought to the complex 'How I imagine 2050 to be with technology and science battling each other' thought, I write it all.
